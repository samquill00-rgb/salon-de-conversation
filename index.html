Présentation

Le Salon
A revival of the literary salon: conversation as a craft, pursued slowly and in good faith, inspired by eighteenth- and nineteenth-century Paris.
L’Esprit
We shall talk, talk and talk; we shall dispute and wonder. I will bring you texts, old and new, some of which you'll know well or half-remember, some of which will be entirely unfamiliar, drawn from the particular store of my own long years of study. Most of all we shall seek to make sense of the world, as we find it now, in the context of the truth and beauty of things past.
